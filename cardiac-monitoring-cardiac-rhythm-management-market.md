<p><p>The cardiac monitoring and rhythm management market encompasses various devices designed to diagnose and manage heart conditions. Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices record heart activity, while Implantable Loop Recorders (ILR) provide long-term monitoring. Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) devices assess heart performance. Event monitors capture arrhythmia episodes, and pacemakers regulate heart rhythms. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices optimize heart function, while defibrillators provide life-saving shocks during cardiac arrest. External defibrillators are portable, and Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) support heart function in severe cases.</p></p>
