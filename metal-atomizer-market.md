<p><ul><li>Gas Metal</li><li>Liquid Metal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal atomizer market encompasses two primary types: gas metal atomizers and liquid metal atomizers. Gas metal atomizers utilize high-pressure gas to disperse molten metal into fine particles, facilitating applications in powder metallurgy and additive manufacturing. Conversely, liquid metal atomizers generate metal powders by injecting a molten metal stream into a controlled environment, often using centrifugal or atomizing techniques. Both methods cater to various industries, providing essential materials for advanced manufacturing processes, enhancing product performance and efficiency.</p></p>
