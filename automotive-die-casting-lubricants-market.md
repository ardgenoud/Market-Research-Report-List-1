<p><p>The automotive die casting lubricants market encompasses several types, including die lubricants and plunger lubricants. Die lubricants are used to reduce friction and improve the release of cast components from the mold, enhancing surface finish and extending tool life. Plunger lubricants, on the other hand, are applied to the plunger mechanism to ensure smooth operation and minimize wear during the injection process. Additional products under "others" may include coatings and release agents tailored for specific applications in die casting.</p></p>
