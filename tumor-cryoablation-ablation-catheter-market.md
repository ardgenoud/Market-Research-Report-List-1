<p><p>The tumor cryoablation catheter market is categorized into two main types: single point and multielectrode catheters. Single point catheters deliver freezing energy at a targeted site, effectively destroying tumor cells in a localized area. In contrast, multielectrode catheters utilize multiple points of contact to simultaneously freeze larger areas, enhancing treatment efficiency for more extensive tumors. Both types aim to minimize damage to surrounding tissues while maximizing the effectiveness of tumor ablation through precise temperature control and application.</p></p>
