<p><ul><li>Halogen Track Lighting</li><li>LED Track Lighting</li><li>Incandescent Track Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial track lighting market encompasses three main types: halogen, LED, and incandescent track lighting. Halogen track lighting offers bright, white light with a longer lifespan than traditional incandescent bulbs, making it energy-efficient while providing excellent color rendering. LED track lighting is the most energy-efficient option, offering long lifespans and low heat output, ideal for versatile commercial applications. Incandescent track lighting, while less efficient, delivers warm light and is often favored for its pleasing ambiance in certain retail or hospitality settings.</p></p>
