<p><p>Hydraulic Liner Hanger Systems are crucial in the oil and gas industry for securing liners in wellbores. The market is divided into two main types: Rotating and Non-Rotating. Rotating Type hangers allow for the rotation of the liner during drilling operations, facilitating better cementing and minimizing borehole damage. In contrast, Non-Rotating Type hangers prevent rotation, providing stability and reducing risks of complications in challenging formations. Both systems enhance well integrity and operational efficiency in various drilling applications.</p></p>
