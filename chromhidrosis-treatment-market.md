<p><strong>The Chromhidrosis Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Apocrine Chromhidrosis</li><li>Eccrine Chromhidrosis</li><li>Pseudochromhidrosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chromhidrosis treatment market comprises three main types: apocrine chromhidrosis, eccrine chromhidrosis, and pseudochromhidrosis. Apocrine chromhidrosis involves discoloration of sweat from apocrine glands, typically in areas like the armpits, requiring treatments like antiperspirants or surgical options. Eccrine chromhidrosis affects eccrine glands, producing colored sweat on broader body areas, often treated with topical agents or lifestyle modifications. Pseudochromhidrosis is a misleading condition where sweat appears colored due to substances in contact, requiring removal of triggers rather than direct treatment for sweat glands.</p></p>
