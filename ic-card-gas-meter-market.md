<p><p>The IC Card Gas Meter market primarily includes diaphragm gas meters and ultrasonic gas meters. Diaphragm gas meters use a flexible diaphragm mechanism to accurately measure gas flow, making them reliable for residential use. Ultrasonic gas meters, on the other hand, utilize ultrasonic sound waves to measure gas flow, offering higher accuracy and fewer moving parts, resulting in lower maintenance. Both types integrate with IC card technology for prepaid functionalities, enhancing user convenience and promoting efficient gas consumption management.</p></p>
