<p><p>The specialty pumps market encompasses several types, including centrifugal, reciprocating, rotary, and diaphragm pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids, ideal for low-viscosity applications. Reciprocating pumps use a back-and-forth motion, effective for high pressure and precision tasks. Rotary pumps transfer fluid through rotating parts and are versatile for various viscosities. Diaphragm pumps operate by flexing a membrane, suitable for handling sensitive or corrosive fluids. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like chemical processing, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
