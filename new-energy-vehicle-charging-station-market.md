<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) charging station market is primarily divided into two types: DC charging piles and AC charging piles. DC charging piles deliver high-speed direct current, enabling faster vehicle charging, typically used for commercial and fleet applications. In contrast, AC charging piles provide alternating current, generally used for residential and public charging solutions, offering slower charging speeds. Both types are essential for supporting the growing demand for electric vehicles, enhancing infrastructure, and promoting clean energy mobility.</p></p>
