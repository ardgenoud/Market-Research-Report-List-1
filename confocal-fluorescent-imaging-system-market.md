<p><ul><li>Laser Scanning Confocal</li><li>Digital Confocal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The confocal fluorescent imaging system market is primarily divided into two types: Laser Scanning Confocal and Digital Confocal systems. Laser Scanning Confocal systems utilize laser light to scan specimens point by point, providing high-resolution images with specific focal depth, ideal for detailed cellular imaging. In contrast, Digital Confocal systems capture images using a digital camera and computational techniques, enabling faster image acquisition and processing while maintaining good resolution. Both types are essential for advanced microscopy applications in biological and material sciences.</p></p>
