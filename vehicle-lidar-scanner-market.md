<p><p>The Vehicle LiDAR Scanner market is mainly categorized into two types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR uses rotating mirrors to scan the environment, offering high-resolution data and a wide field of view, making it suitable for various applications in autonomous vehicles. In contrast, Solid State LiDAR employs stationary components and uses electronic scanning techniques, resulting in a more compact design, enhanced reliability, and lower production costs, appealing for integration into modern vehicle systems.</p></p>
