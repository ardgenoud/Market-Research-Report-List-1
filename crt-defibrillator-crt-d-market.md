<p><p>The CRT Defibrillator (CRT-D) market encompasses devices designed to manage heart rhythm disorders, particularly in heart failure patients. It includes two primary types: internal defibrillators, which are implanted within the body to deliver shocks directly to the heart when needed, enhancing cardiac function and preventing sudden cardiac arrest; and external defibrillators, which are user-friendly, portable devices that administer shocks externally. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes and are essential in emergency cardiac care and heart failure management.</p></p>
