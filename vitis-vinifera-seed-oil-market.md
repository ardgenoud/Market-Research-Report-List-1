<p><p>The Vitis Vinifera seed oil market is categorized into three primary extraction methods. Mechanical pressing involves physically crushing the seeds to extract oil, offering a natural and minimally processed product. Solvent extraction uses chemical solvents to dissolve and separate the oil, enhancing yield but potentially introducing residues. Carbon dioxide supercritical extraction employs pressurized CO2 as a solvent to selectively extract oil, ensuring high purity and preserving bioactive compounds. Each method impacts the oil’s quality, flavor, and health benefits.</p></p>
