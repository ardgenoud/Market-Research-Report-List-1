<p><ul><li>Metal Matrix</li><li>Ceramic Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer Composite market includes various types, notably Metal Matrix Composites (MMCs) and Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMCs). MMCs combine carbon fibers with metal matrices, enhancing strength and thermal conductivity, and are used in aerospace and automotive applications. CMCs, on the other hand, integrate carbon fibers with ceramic matrices, offering high-temperature resistance and strength, ideal for aerospace, military, and industrial applications. Both types leverage carbon fiber's lightweight and high-strength properties to improve performance and durability in demanding environments.</p></p>
