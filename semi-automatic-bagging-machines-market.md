<p><p>The semi-automatic bagging machines market features two primary types: horizontal and vertical bagging machines. Horizontal bagging machines operate by placing bags horizontally on a conveyor system, allowing for easy access and efficient filling, ideal for bulk materials. In contrast, vertical bagging machines fill and seal bags in an upright position, which is better suited for granules, powders, and small items, optimizing space and enhancing speed. Both types enhance packaging efficiency across various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture.</p></p>
