These materials address the critical challenge of silicon's volumetric expansion during charge-discharge cycles, making them suitable for high-performance applications in electric vehicles, portable electronics, and renewable energy storage systems.</p><p>The porous silicon–carbon anode material market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for efficient energy storage solutions and advancements in battery technologies.
