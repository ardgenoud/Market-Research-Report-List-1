<p><p>The therapeutic laser systems market encompasses various types including diode lasers, solid-state lasers, dye lasers, and gas lasers. Diode laser systems are compact and efficient, ideal for targeted treatments. Solid-state lasers utilize crystals for high energy output and versatility. Dye laser systems are known for their tunability and effectiveness in treating specific conditions. Gas lasers, utilizing gaseous mediums, are often employed in larger applications and offer unique properties for various therapeutic procedures. Each type serves distinct medical and aesthetic applications.</p></p>
