<p><p>Swimwear fabrics primarily include polyester, PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate), and nylon. Polyester is durable, quick-drying, and resistant to fading, making it a popular choice for swimwear. PBT offers superior elasticity, stretchability, and chlorine resistance, ideal for competitive swimwear. Nylon, known for its softness and comfort, provides excellent fit and flexibility but may not be as chlorine-resistant as the others. Each fabric type caters to different needs, ranging from recreational to professional swimming.</p></p>
