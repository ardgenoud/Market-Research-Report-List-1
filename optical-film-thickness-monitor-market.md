<p><strong>The Optical Film Thickness Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflectivity Method</li><li>Intervention</li><li>Laser Scattering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Film Thickness Monitor market encompasses three primary types: Reflectivity Method, Intervention, and Laser Scattering. The Reflectivity Method utilizes the principle of light reflection to measure film thickness, providing real-time data by analyzing changes in reflectance. Intervention methods involve direct manipulation or adjustment of the film process to ensure optimal thickness, often relying on real-time feedback. Laser Scattering employs laser technology to detect film thickness by analyzing scattered light patterns, offering high precision in various applications.</p></p>
