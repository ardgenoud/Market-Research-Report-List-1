<p><p>The polylactic acid (PLA) market includes three main types: Poly(L-lactic) acid, Poly(D-lactic) acid, and Poly(DL-lactic) acid. Poly(L-lactic) acid is primarily used for its biocompatibility and is popular in medical applications. Poly(D-lactic) acid offers higher crystallinity and thermal stability, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Poly(DL-lactic) acid is a racemic mixture that provides a balance of properties, ideal for certain biodegradable uses. The growth of sustainable materials drives demand across these segments in various industries.</p></p>
