<p><p>The survey software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based survey software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easy accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection. It typically operates on a subscription model. In contrast, on-premises survey software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring significant IT resources and infrastructure. Organizations choose between them based on their specific needs and security preferences.</p></p>
