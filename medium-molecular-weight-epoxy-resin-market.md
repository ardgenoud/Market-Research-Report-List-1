<p><p>The Medium Molecular Weight Epoxy Resin market comprises three main types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity epoxy resins. Low viscosity epoxy resins offer excellent flow properties, making them ideal for applications requiring intricate detail or penetration. Medium viscosity resins strike a balance between workability and strength, suitable for general-purpose applications. High viscosity epoxy resins provide superior thickness and are often used in structural applications, where added strength and durability are paramount. Each type serves specific industrial needs effectively.</p></p>
