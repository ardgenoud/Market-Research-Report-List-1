<p><p>The water-based inorganic zinc-rich coating market is divided into two primary types: self-curing and heat curing. Self-curing coatings harden through moisture and ambient humidity, making them suitable for various environments without additional heat application. In contrast, heat-curing coatings require elevated temperatures to achieve optimal hardness and adhesion, often providing enhanced performance in aggressive conditions. Both types are utilized in protective applications, including industrial and marine settings, due to their superior corrosion resistance and environmental benefits.</p></p>
