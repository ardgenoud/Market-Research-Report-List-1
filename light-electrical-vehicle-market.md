<p><p>The light electric vehicle market includes three main types: </p><p>1. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, improving fuel efficiency while retaining traditional refueling capabilities.  </p><p>2. Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) also feature both power sources but can be charged from external power outlets, allowing for greater electric-only range.  </p><p>3. Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) run solely on electric power, relying on charging stations, offering zero tailpipe emissions and a fully electric driving experience. </p></p>
