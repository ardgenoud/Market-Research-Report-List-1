<p><p>The PVC floor market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Homogeneous**: Consists of a single layer with uniform composition, offering high durability and easy maintenance. </p><p>2. **Heterogeneous**: Composed of multiple layers, it provides varied designs and improved acoustic qualities. </p><p>3. **Vinyl Tiles (VT)**: Available in various designs and colors, these tiles are easy to install and maintain. </p><p>4. **Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT)**: High-end versions that imitate natural materials, offering superior aesthetics and resilience, ideal for both residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
