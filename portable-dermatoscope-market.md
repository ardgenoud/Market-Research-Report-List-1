<p><p>The portable dermatoscope market is primarily divided into two types: traditional and digital dermatoscopes. Traditional dermatoscopes utilize optical systems to provide visual examinations of skin lesions, relying on magnification and illumination. In contrast, digital dermatoscopes leverage advanced imaging technology to capture high-resolution images that can be analyzed and stored electronically. Digital models facilitate remote consultations and enhance diagnostic capabilities, making them increasingly popular among dermatologists for improved patient care and data management.</p></p>
