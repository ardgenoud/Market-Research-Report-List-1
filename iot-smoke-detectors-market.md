<p><strong>The IOT Smoke Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li><li>Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT smoke detector market is categorized into three main types: photoelectric, ionization, and combination detectors. Photoelectric detectors utilize a light source to detect smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization detectors, on the other hand, use radioactive material to sense fast-burning flames. Combination detectors integrate both technologies, offering comprehensive protection by detecting a wider range of fire types. The growing demand for smart home solutions is driving the adoption of these advanced smoke detection systems.</p></p>
