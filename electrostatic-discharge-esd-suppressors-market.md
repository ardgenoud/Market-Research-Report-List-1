<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) suppressors play a crucial role in protecting electronic components from voltage spikes. The market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Diode Arrays**: These consist of multiple diodes in a single package, offering robust protection by clamping excess voltage.</p><p>2. **Polymer**: Polymer-based suppressors are lightweight and flexible, providing a compact solution with high surge tolerance.</p><p>3. **TVS Devices**: Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) devices respond quickly to voltage spikes, effectively shunting excess energy away from sensitive components to ensure their safety.</p></p>
