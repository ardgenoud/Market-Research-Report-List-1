<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Furniture Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particleboard (PB)</li><li>Medium density fibreboard (MDF)</li><li>High density fibreboard (HDF)</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li><li>Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture panels market encompasses various types, each with distinct characteristics. Particleboard (PB) is cost-effective and made from wood chips, suitable for low-end furniture. Medium density fibreboard (MDF) offers smoother surfaces for painting and veneering, making it ideal for more refined applications. High density fibreboard (HDF) is denser and more durable, often used in flooring and high-stress furniture. Oriented strand board (OSB) is engineered from layered wood strands, providing strength and versatility. Plywood consists of thin veneers glued together, renowned for its stability and structural integrity in furniture design.</p></p>
