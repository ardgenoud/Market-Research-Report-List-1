<p><strong>The Fire Alarm Control Panel FACP Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional</li><li>Addressable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fire Alarm Control Panel (FACP) market comprises two main types: conventional and addressable systems. Conventional systems categorize devices into zones, making it challenging to pinpoint the exact location of a fire. They are simpler and more cost-effective for smaller applications. In contrast, addressable systems assign unique identifiers to each device, allowing precise location identification and quicker response times. This advanced technology enhances overall safety and is preferred for larger, more complex installations where rapid detection is crucial.</p></p>
