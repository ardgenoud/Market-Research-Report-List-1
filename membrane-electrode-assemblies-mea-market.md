<p><p>Membrane Electrode Assemblies (MEAs) are essential components of fuel cells, comprising layers that facilitate the electrochemical reactions. The 3-layer MEA typically includes a proton exchange membrane (PEM) and two electrode layers, making it suitable for low-power applications. The 5-layer MEA adds more complexity with additional catalyst layers for improved efficiency. The 7-layer MEA further optimizes performance with enhanced diffusion and reaction zones, catering to high-performance fuel cells in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
